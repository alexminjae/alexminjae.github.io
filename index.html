Automated Torque-to-Failure Analysis

Tesla · Materials Engineering Intern

Developed a Python-based tool to automate torque–angle interpretation for fastener validation testing, replacing manual curve inspection.

Implemented slope-based yield detection with percentage drop thresholds and angular constraints to reliably identify yield, snug, and peak prevailing torque across multiple data formats.

Piezo Sensor Protection Cap

Tesla · Materials Engineering Intern

Designed and productionized protective caps for piezoelectric sensors used in vehicle durability testing, ensuring reliable data collection in wet and high-impact environments.

The primary challenge was balancing full water sealing with fast, tool-less installation while supporting multiple bolt sizes and vehicle configurations. To address this, I developed a parametric cap system that automatically adapts to bolt geometry, enabling rapid generation of variants without redesign.

The final solution was mass-produced and deployed across durability instrumentation builds, supporting multiple drive types and achieving consistent waterproof performance with quick hand-press installation.
