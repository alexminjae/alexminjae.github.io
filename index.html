Context: The Next-Gen Vehicle program required durability testing where fasteners with built-in piezo sensors had to be shielded from mud and high-pressure water.

01. Challenge

In-line fastener validation for the next-gen platform faced bottlenecks due to manual data analysis. Engineers were manually picking "yield," "snug," and "peak" points on torque-angle traces, which was slow and prone to human error.

02. Solution

Global Deployment: Designed the tool to interface with standard factory hardware, allowing deployment to Gigafactories in Reno, Berlin, and Shanghai.

03. Impact

Efficiency: Reduced analysis cycle time by 30+ minutes per joint.

Deliverable: Automated PDF reporting allows line engineers to visualize pass/fail criteria instantly on the production floor.

ReAX Transport Fixtures

PTC Creo GD&T 3D Printing

01. Challenge

Risk: Critical connector joints on ReAX steering systems were sustaining damage during international shipments to Germany and China.

Requirement: Needed a custom fixture that could protect the exposed connectors from impact and vibration while fitting within standard shipping containers.

02. Action

Design: I designed three unique protective fixtures in PTC Creo tailored to the ReAX geometry. I utilized GD&T to ensure the internal clearances were tight enough to prevent movement but loose enough for easy manual installation.

Manufacturing: 3D printed prototypes using FDM to verify fit-up on the physical units before approving the final shipment configuration.

03. Impact

  • Ensured 100% successful delivery of prototype units to international facilities.
  • Prevented project delays caused by shipping damage.

Formula SAE Aerodynamics

SolidWorks ANSYS CFD Composites

01. Challenge

The racing team needed to increase cornering speeds without compromising straight-line acceleration. The existing aero package generated insufficient downforce relative to the drag penalty.

02. Action

Simulation: I modeled multi-element front and rear wings in SolidWorks and performed iterative CFD simulations in ANSYS Fluent to optimize the angle of attack and flap geometry.

Fabrication: Engineered the physical wing structure using a carbon fiber skin over foam cores with machined aluminum mounting points for rigidity.

03. Impact

  • Optimized the lift-to-drag ratio, achieving a 30% drag reduction while maintaining target downforce.
  • Established a standardized CFD workflow for future aerodynamic sub-teams.

Pipsqueak Stirling Engine

Siemens NX Machining GD&T

01. Challenge

To design and manufacture a fully functional thermal-to-mechanical energy conversion system (Stirling Engine) from scratch. The main difficulty was achieving the extremely tight air-tight tolerances required for the piston cylinder without industrial-grade precision equipment.

02. Action

Modeling: I modeled 18 distinct components in Siemens NX, applying strict GD&T controls to critical mating surfaces.

Materials & Assembly: I utilized a combination of brass, nylon, and polished metal to manage friction and thermal expansion. I spent over 260 hours machining and assembling the components into a custom "Pirate Ship" form factor.

03. Impact

  • Achieved a fully functional engine that runs continuously on a simple heat differential.
  • Demonstrated mastery of complex assembly modeling and manual machining tolerances.

Pagani Huayra Components

Siemens NX Class-A Surfacing Reverse Engineering

01. Challenge

To reverse-engineer and replicate the complex, Class-A organic surfaces of the Pagani Huayra Roadster's wheel spokes and exhaust manifold using only reference images.

02. Action

Advanced Surfacing: I utilized Siemens NX advanced surfacing tools to recreate the curvature-continuous geometry. I analyzed the topology to ensure the surfaces were manufacturable.

Material Selection: Designed the components assuming Iron 40 and lightweight alloy properties to simulate realistic weight distribution.

03. Impact

  • Produced a high-fidelity CAD model suitable for rendering and manufacturing analysis.
  • Refined skills in complex surface modeling and reverse engineering workflows.
