ZF — ReAX Connector Transport Protection

Mechanical Design Engineering

Designed protective transport fixtures for ReAX connector assemblies to prevent joint damage during international shipment.

Developed connector-specific designs to constrain joints under handling and vibration without introducing stress concentrations.

Fixtures were successfully used during shipments to Germany and China, eliminating connector damage and rework.
