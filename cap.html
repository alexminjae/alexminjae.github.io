Piezo Sensor Protection Cap

Designed waterproof protection caps for piezo sensors used in vehicle durability testing, addressing repeated failures caused by water ingress during long-term vehicle tests.

Created parametric CATIA V6 Powercopy models to automatically generate cap geometries based on bolt size and drive type, enabling rapid iteration across multiple variants.

Validated designs through material testing (Nylon, ASA, TPU), hand-insertion trials, and water resistance testing, achieving full sealing performance.
